Purpose/Theme

The Desert Glory Theme is inspired by the love of the deserts plank canvas. You can take shape through an assortment of earth tone color pallets reminiscent of a desert landscape and create complex css animation that bring life. Simple theme can have a flourishing environment steeped in plant and animal illustrations.
